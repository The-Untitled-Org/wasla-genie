## What Are Meetings of Mind?

Meetings of Mind (MoMs) capture the thinking behind architectural decisions, trade-offs, and strategic choices. They're not just action items — they document the *why* behind what we build.

Each MoM includes:
- Key decisions made
- Implementation priorities
- Open questions
- Phased rollout plans
- Design rationale

Check back regularly as we add new MoMs from ongoing development sessions.
